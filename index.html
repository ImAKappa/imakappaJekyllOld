Turtle Facts

Turtles are one of the best animals

Their shells develop from their rib cage

The largest turtles are Leather Back sea turtles. They can reach up to 2m and can weigh up to 2000lbs

The smallest turtle is the Speckled Padloper turtle, about 8cm long and no more than 140g. They are found in South Africa.
