Easy

Most intervals smaller than an octave. Very little syncopation.

Medium

Octaves. Requires independent hand coordination. Moderate speeds.

Intermediate

Octaves. A couple months to a few years piano experience. Syncopation. Arpeggios.

Hard

Larger-than-Octave intervals. May be quite fast. Hands may jump around a bit. Multiple voices with one hand (requires dynamic control and independent finger control). Larger hands would help too.

Advanced

Potentially everything listed in Hard + complex passages, hand crossing, may be a longer piece. Chords in quick succession. Several years experience recommended.
